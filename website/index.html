Digital closet

Photograph and organize items by category, season, price, rating, and how often you wear them. Add as many pieces as you like.

Outfit builder

Arrange pieces on a drag-and-resize canvas or upload a photo of a complete look. Plan unlimited outfits.

OOTD calendar

Schedule outfits by date and optionally note the temperature for weather-aware planning.

Smart filters

Sort and filter by season, category, wear frequency, purchase date, and more.

JSON backup

Export and import your wardrobe data so you stay in control of your closet archive.

Your closet stays on your device

Wardrobe is built local-first. Your wardrobe photos, outfits, and calendar data are stored on your phone. Core features work offline. Optional inspiration browsing loads third-party web content and may use network access.
